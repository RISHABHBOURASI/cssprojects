Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Missile Man of India & 11th President of India

About

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam (1931–2015) was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. Known as the "Missile Man of India" for his role in developing ballistic missile and launch vehicle technology, he inspired millions with his vision for India's development.

Achievements

  • Played a major role in India’s Pokhran-II nuclear tests in 1998.
  • Served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007.
  • Awarded Bharat Ratna, Padma Bhushan, and Padma Vibhushan.
  • Authored inspiring books such as *Wings of Fire* and *Ignited Minds*.
